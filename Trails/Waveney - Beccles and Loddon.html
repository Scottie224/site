Waveney Valley Food Trail

The Chet & Waveney Valley Vineyard

This is a premium winery making the youthful wines of prizewinning winemaker, John Hemmant. The chet and Waveney Calley Anteros wine is produced in Norfolk, with wines aged from one to five years. This ensures that the highest quality wine is produced, with more layers of fruit and more bubbles.

Chet & Waveney Valley Vineyard, Loddon Roa, Bergh Apton, Norfolk, NR15 1BT, United Kingdom

01508 480679

Hundred River Farm

The farm is within the Waveney Valley environmentally sensitive area, so their traditional farming methods enables an abundance of wildlife to flourish. At this farm, the cattle mature slowly to ensure that only top quality beef is produced, along with smooth, creamy milk for their ice cream, yoghurt, milk and cream.

Valley Farm, Sotterley, Beccles, Suffolk, NR34 7UH

01502 476063 / 07934570235

Maisebrooke Farm

This Farm has a small farm shop, which sells tasty meat and produce direct to the public. They breed and rear their own pure Saddleback Pigs, wild boar and Cameroon sheep, which is sold exclusively in their shop. But, their range of products is not extensive, so care is taken to get as many products as possible from local and reputable sources.

Maisebrooke Farm, Shipmeadow, Beccles, Suffolk, NR34 8HJ

01502 711018

Rosy Lees Tearoom

The aim of Rosy Lees Tearoom is to use local produce. They stay a key part of the community by using only local suppliers to obtain their produce.

37A Bridge Street, Loddon Norwich, Norfolk, NR14 6NA

01508 520204

Samphire

Samphire is an award-winning smallholding in Norfolk that rears a rare breed of pigs and sheep. Ethical farming is a priority of Samphire, and their commitment to animal welfare has been recognised by the RSPCA.

Samphire Farm Shop, Sycamore Farm, Pristow Green Lane, Tibenham, Norfolk, NR16 1PU

01379 674413

The Flying Chef

The speciality of The Flying Chef is the traditional chargrilled sausages, made with chump chop of local pork, served in a poppy seed roll with a choice of onions, lettuce, mayonnaise and sauces, including their legendary home-made hot sweet chilli relish.

07733 227484

WJ Seppings Butchers

This team of butchers are highly trained and knowledgeable in how to cut and prepare meat and do so on the premises. Wherever possible, all of their meat is locally sourced, with all pork coming from a South Norfolk Farm. Despite making over a ton and a half of their famous sausages every week, the sausages are still made to a high standard by following the recipe which has been handed down through the generations.

W.J. Seppings Ltd, 9 Hungate, Beccles, Suffolk, NR34 9TT

01502 712188
