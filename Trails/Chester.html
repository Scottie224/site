Chester Ale Trail

Bawn Lodge

Located a short 15 minute walk from Chester’s city centre, The Lodge Bar has many ales available, with over 400 different ales stocked last year alone. The bar buys direct from many different breweries across the country.

8-10 Hoole Road, Chester, CH2 3NH

01244324971

Bear and Billet

This friendly, relaxing atmosphere is the place of home cooked food and a wide selection of real ales and bottles beers from all around the world, with live football, rugby and cricket to name a few for entertainment.

94 Lower Bridge Street, Chester, Cheshire, CH1 1RU

01244 311886

The Brewery Tap

Determined to offer the best and widest selection of cask ales, the Brewery Tap offers a range of guest ales, traditional hand-pull cider and, of course, a choice of beers from Chester’s own brewery – Spitting Feathers.

The Brewery Tap, 52-54 Lower Bridge Street, Chester, CH1 1RU

01244 340999

The Coach House Inn

This nineteenth century city centre pub serves quality, country kitchen cooking with traditional ales and excellent wines. Proud of how much care and attention the give to managing their cellar, and serving the beers exactly as the brewers intended.

The Coach House, 39 Northgate Street, Chester, CH1 2HQ

01244 351900

Mill Hotel and Spa

The Real Ale Bar of the Hotel is located alongside the Shropshire Union Canal on the ground floor and is featured in Good Beer Guide 1994-2011. Serving a great selection of Real Ales, with 16 hand pumps, 10 regularly changing Cask Ales, over 2000 guest beers per year, a live jazz band every Monday and a Deli Counter that serves hot bar means, salads, jacket potatoes, sandwiches and baguettes.

Mill Hotel & Spa Destination, Mill Hotel Ltd, Milton Street, Chester, CH1 3NF

01244 350035

Old Harkers Arms

This old city of London boozer has a really good reputation for its food, great meeting place over a pub lunch with no doubt that half of the commercial and professional heart of the city will be in there at some point during the week.

The Old Harkers Arms, 1 Russell Street, Chester, Cheshire, CH3 5AL

02144 344 525

Pied Bull

This is the only working brewery within Chester, less than 50 yards from the site of the famous Northgate Brewery, won a CAMRA award winning beer in October 2012. The range of ales here is quickly expanding, combining the best of brewing tradition with contemporary styles.

57 Northgate Street, Chester, CH1 2HO

01244 325829

Ring o' Bells

Ring O’Bells is a welcoming Cheshire pub combining high class food with the finest wines and beers that hosts regular events including Farmers Markets so you can try even more local cuisine.

Ring O’Bells, Village Rd, Christleton, Chester, CH3 7AS

01244 335422

Telford's Warehouse

Renowned as a music and arts venue, Telford's Warehouse offers an exceptional selection of European beers, CAMRA rated cask conditioned ales and an extensive wine list. Superbly located on the edge of the canal, Telford's offers a fantastic beer garden or grand indoor seating.

Telford’s Warehouse, Tower Wharf, Chester, CH1 4EZ

01244 390 090

Temple Bar

Situated in the centre of Chester, close to the nightclubs, this is a popular circuit pub with a range of drinks found at the bar. Food is available every day until early evening with a menu full of many choices.

Frodsham Street, Chester, CH1 3JL

01244 328007

Kash Taprooms

Kash Taprooms is an American Style bar selling American Craft beers and Cask Ales, such as Dead Pony Club, Blue Monkey, Hophead, Oakham, Sandstone, and from the local brewery, Frodsham Brewery, Spank 6%.

121 Brook Street, Chester, Cheshire, CH1 3DU

01244 401777
