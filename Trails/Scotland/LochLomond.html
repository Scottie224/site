Loch Lomond

Martin Wishart at Loch Lomond

Since opening this restaurants in 1999, Martin Wishart has established himself as one of the UK’s most highly respected chefs, currently holding one Michelin star in two restaurants, 4 AA rosettes and a score of 8/10 in the Good Food Guide.

Cameron House, Loch Lomond, G83 8QZ

01389 722 504

Allanwater Brewhouse

The Allanwater Brewhouse if where all the cask conditioned real ales and bottles ales are produced. Selling a variety from bottled beer, cask ales and a lot of merchandise both in house and online. They have a strong ethos of only using the most natural and ethical ingredients and processes.

Allanwater Brewhouse, Queens Lane, Bridge of Allan, Stirlingshire, FK9 4HP

01786 834555

Deanston Distillery

Deanston Distillery sits by the River Teith at the entrance to the Loch Lomond and Trossachs National Park. Deanston was transformed into a distillery in the 1960’s and has been distilling whisky using traditional methods ever since. The whisky is described as sweet, fruity with a malt honeyed spiciness on the palate.

8 Milton Road, College Milton North, East Kilbride, G74 5BU

01355 260999

The Birds & Bees

The Birds and Bees is situated just outside Stirling main centre with a full view of The Wallace Monument. This is a multiple award-winning restaurant, bar and function suite venue that has won Scotland’s top Pub award in the past.

The Birds and the Bees Restaurant, Easter Cornton Road, Causewayhead, Stirling, FK9 5PB

01786 473663

Glengoyne Distillery

Glengoyne is just 40 minutes drive from Glasgow or 30 minutes from Stirling and Loch Lomond. Glengoyne offers a range of diverse distillery tours, which are open all year round for guided tours, whisky tastings, in-depth distillery visits, private dining and a whisky shop.

Dumgoyne, Near Killearn, Glasgow, G63 9LB

01360 550254

The Lade Inn

The Lade Inn pride themselves in using fresh and the best locally source ingredients, whether that is their handmade beef burgers, fresh trout from Trossach’s Trout farm or their fresh vegetables. They also take pride in their real ale that is available and even have their own recipe House ales Waylade, Ladeback and Ladeout, which are brewed by Traditional Scottish Ales in Stirling.

The Lade Inn, Kilmahog, Callander, FK17 8HD

01877 330152

The Ardardan Estate and Farm Shop

They strongly believe in traceability and keeping their food miles to a minimum, so they source their produce from local Scottish suppliers where possible. With a background in farming, they understand the important in home grown and local produce. This is why the products they stock are speciality products and not just local supermarket food. They also offer a range of fresh fruit and vegetables and free range eggs.

Ardardan Estate, Cardross, Argyll & Bute, G82 5HD

01389 849188
