Outer Hebrides Food and Drink

Hebridean Smokehouse

Located on the Isle of North Uist and is part of the Outer Hebridean archipelago west of the Scottish mainland. Having been smoking locally reared and landed fish and shellfish for nearly 30 years, it is no surprise that their smoked salmon has been described by Prue Leith as “the best in the world”. The clean waters support all the salmon and sea trout used to make their special peat and beechwood smoked products. The small, local inshore fishing fleet land is also used to produce the very best scallops for them to smoke with locally cut peat.

Hebridean Smokehouse Ltd Clachan Isle of North Uist Scotland HS6 5HD

01876 580209

Charles Macleod Family

A family business that stretches back over 6 decades has made its success built firmly on two established principles, which are top quality meat and excellent customer service and attention to detail. Their meat is guaranteed to be the best produce with the rich island pastures and an environment that few can surpass. Also, from knowing their suppliers personally, you can certify that they only ever use the best products around.

Charles Macleod Ropework Park, Stornoway, Isle of Lewis, HS1 2LB

01851 702445

Macleans Bakery

Macleans Bakery sells a wide range of breads, confectionery and crisp handmade oatcakes, made to traditional recipes. This is a small independent bakery that prides itself on the quality of its products, with the owners remaining personally involved in the baking of all their oatcakes and biscuits.

Isle Of Benbecula Outer Hebrides HS7 5LY

01870 602659

Hebridean Brewing Company

This is the only brewery and legal alcohol producer in the Outer Hebrides that offers locally produced cask conditioned real ales. The owner, Andy, realised that real ale produced locally would add to the diversity of beers available on the island.

18a Bell’s Road Stornoway Isle of Lewis Western Isles

Ardbhan Organics

Ardbhan Organics specialises in Organic Highland beef, Hebridean mutton and potatoes. The welfare of the animals is very important, so the animals are managed by this family enterprise from birth to plate.

Whiteshore Kyles, Paible, North Uist, HS6 5DY

01876 510293

Hebridean Toffee

Hebridean Toffee is a luxury Scottish tablet, which is entirely hand-made on the Isle of Barra in the Outer Hebrides of Scotland. The factory is situated in Castlebay, overlooking Kisimul Castle. The Toffee is made to order and is usually shipped within 24 hours and guarantees the full 6 month shelf life.

W J Macdonald

Stornoway Black Pudding has been made at WJ MacDonald butcher’s shop since 1931. One of the most unique examples of Scottish fayre. Their black puddings combine traditional fresh ingredients with a blend of spices to produce a pudding full of flavour.

5 Francis St, Stornoway, Isle Of Lewis, HS1 2XD

01851 702077

Stags Bakeries

All of the products sold here are created from scratch in their Stornoway bakery. The finest ingredients are selected, with the quality being very important throughout the entire process, to ensure that every biscuit is a good as possible. They offer a few types of biscuits, including savoury, sweet, specials and seaweed.

Stag Bakeries Ltd 8 Cannery Road Stornoway Isle of Lewis Scotland HS1 2SF
