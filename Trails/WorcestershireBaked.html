Baked Goods and Confectionary

Fudge Heaven

Started in 2001 and owned and run by Gary Davis who makes his own recipe goodies. The fudge is available in almost 20 flavours, from traditional ‘Bustin Vanilla’ to ‘Bailey’s Comet’, which is made using real Bailey’s liqueur.

Ye Olde Stable, Metal and Ores Industrial Estate, Hanbury Road, Stoke Prior Bromsgrove, B60 4JZ

01527 832595

Loads of Waffle

The caterer provides freshly cooked waffles and pancakes, topped with a choice of sauces, whipped cream and fruit. Fresh fruit is sourced locally when in season and the equipment used to cook the waffles can be used indoor and outdoor, making it the ideal place for events.

28 North Road, Evesham, WR11 3AY

07929 137123

Mr Chili's Traditional Sweet Emporium

An old-fashioned style sweet shop where you can choose from a range of 300 jars of sweets, including their specially made plum and custard sweets, Evesham rock, 14 varieties of hand-made fudge, handmade English and Continental Chocolates and up to 32 varieties of liquorice.

57 Bridge Street, Evesham, WR11 4SG

01386 429000

Ombersley Bakery

This is a family owned bakery, providing artisan bread, patés, quiches and many other baked products.

Main Road, Ombersley, Worcestershire, WR9 0DP

01905 620350

The Pudding Shop

This family-run business is involved in every aspect of production, from developing new flavours and sourcing ingredients, local ones where possible, right through to production and sales. These British puddings are available in the post, from stalls on the Farmer’s Markets and Food Festivals, as well as the growing number of suppliers, restaurants, cafes and local shops.

14D Backfields, Upton-upon-Severn, WR8 0JH

01684 593867
