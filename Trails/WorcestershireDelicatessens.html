Delicatessens

Deli 6

Pride themselves in hand-made cakes and treats, also catering for special diets, such as egg and gluten free. From the deli counter, many products are available, including, hand-cut coleslaw, ham, cheeses, butter and drinks. Deli 6 also sells many imported breads, including olive ciabatta, sour dough and rye bread and the shelves are stocked with many preserves, chutneys, pickles, mustards, dressings and oils.

6 High Street, Droitwich Spa, Worcestershire, WR9 8EW

01905 773381

The Deli at Hodgehill

Open since 2009, this Deli sells quality produce based on a passion for fine food. The objective is to provide a range of products that fuse together the locally sourced meat and farm produce provide at the local Hodgehill Farm.

Birmingham Road, Kidderminster, Worcestershire, DY10 3NR

07773 004992

The Great Malvern Deli

Specialising in serving fresh, home-made, light meals to eat in or take away. Most of the ingredients and produced are prepared on-site, whilst they also supply many local products, such as cheeses and ambient goods that are unavailable in the average supermarket.

11 Abbey Road, Great Malvern, Worcestershire, WR14 3ES

01684 899091
