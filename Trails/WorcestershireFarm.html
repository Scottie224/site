Farm Shops and Local Producers

Array Fruit Farm

This Farm Shop opened in 1958 and continues to sell a wide range of fresh and local produce, including fruit and vegetables, meat, eggs, preserves, honey, cakes and biscuits.

Perryfields Road, Bromsgrove, B61 8QN

01527 875888

Ellenden Farm Shop

This is a family-run, award-winning farm shop that specialises in highly reputable home-bred beef and lamb, local free-range chicken and pork. The family is passionate about sourcing and providing local fresh produce, selling home-grown seasonal fruit and vegetables, their own free-range eggs, local bread, cakes, preserves, beverages and dairy produce.

Evesham Road, Harvington, Evesham, WR11 8LU

01386 870296

Gwilliams Farm Shop

This is a small family-run Farm with a Farm Shop, where the owners pride themselves on the quality and freshness of their produce. Grows seasonal fruit and vegetables and their beef is reared from calves, they also sell locally produced cakes, pies, pickles, chutneys, drinks, dairy products and meats.

Ombersley Road, Claines, Worcester, WR3 7RH

01905 756490

Hodgehill Farm Shop

This Farm Shop sells it all, from fresh fruit and vegetables, to pickles and jams, from home-grown flowers to bedding and houseplants, from seeds and bulbs to roses and trees.

Birmingham Road, Kidderminster, Worcestershire, DY10 3NR

01562 822058

Hopleys Farm Shop

Selling fresh and seasonal produce from across the region. Locally sourced plants, meat, dairy products, sweet foods, preserves and local beverages.

Cleobury Road, Bewdley, Worcestershire, DY12 2QL

01299 404826

Meadwell Farm Shop

This is a family-run Farm Shop, selling home-grown and locally sourced produce. They also have a Veg Box delivery service within Evesham and surrounding villages. Jams, chutneys and cakes are made on-site and juices and dairy that are also available to buy.

Cleeve Road, Middle Littleton, Evesham, WR11 8JR

01386 834241

M J Freemantle

This working farm, located near Pershore, produces an assortment of fruit and vegetables, including pumpkins, squashes, gourds and plums, which are all sold direct from the farm.

The Holding, Smokey Lane, Cropthorne, Pershore, WR10 3NF

01386 860151

Old Yarr Estates Farm Shop

This Farm Shop stands on 300 acres of land, towards Freckenham, where their food is sourced. The shop sells fresh produce and a variety of locally sourced beverages, along with the new delicatessen which offers many cellar-matured cheeses, complements and condiments and freshly baked bread and dressings.

Astwood Lane, Astwood Bank, Redditch, B96 6PS

01527 894167

Revills Farm Shop

Opening just 8 years ago, this Farm Shop has a reputation for home grown fruit and vegetables. The shop is stocked by local produce, including meat and dairy products, preserves, baked items and beverages.

Bourne Road, Defford, NR. Worcester, WR8 9BS

01386 750466

Sutton House Farm Shop

This is a mixed family farm with cows, sheep, potatoes, thatching straw and fruit, which are used to produce their own juices. A regular supply of honey is maintained through beekeeping that has been part of the business for 30 years.

Sutton House, Sutton, Tenbury Wells, Worcestershire, WR15 8RJ

01584 810424

Wayside Farm Shop

This traditional farm shop and tearoom supports local growers and independent food producers. They sell seasonal fruit and vegetables, dairy products, sausages and pies. Stocking local honey, beer and cider, they also produce their own range of preserves and home-baked goodies. Flowers and plants are also available and made to order hampers. This Farm Shop has featured in BBC Countryfile and Bill’s Tasty Weekends.

50 Pitchers Hill, Wickhamford, Evesham, WR11 7RT

01386 830546
