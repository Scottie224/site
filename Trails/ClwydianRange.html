The Clwydian Range Food Trail

Caffi Florence

Fresh, local, seasonal, homemade food. A taste of wales with great food, all homemade from scratch using the best locally source, seasonal ingredients. Day menu includes breakfast and brunch section, homemade soups, main and light lunches, desserts, traditional English and Welsh afternoon teas and a great range of cakes and quality teas and fairtrade coffee. They specialise in making the best afternoon tea by using leaves in real tea pots to make a seriously good cup of tea. Featured on BBC’s Rip Off Britain with great review and compliments.

Loggerheads Country Park, Ruthin Road, Loggerheads, Denbighshire, CH7 5LH

01352 810397

Rhesgoad Farm Shop

A family run business located on a working farm in an area of natural beauty. They produce the finest Welsh Lamb and Pork in the Clwydian Range. They offer locally produced products of poultry and beef along with homemade sausages from their home reared pork.

Rhesgoed Farm, Llanbedr Dyffryn Clwyd, Ruthin, LL151YE

01824703090

WG Jones

WG Jones offers beautiful cuts of meat and they pride themselves in quality bacon and sausages and have won True Taste Awards. They also feature great cotemporary classics and hot dishes from the extensive WG Jones menu.

Well Street, Ruthin, LL151AF

01824 702807

Leonardo's Delicatessen

Purveyors of fine food & wines, specialising in bespoke takeaways and outside catering. Leonardo’s Delicatessen has won several national awards including not only, Champion Pie (with their Chicken, Leek & Laver bread pie) at the British Pie awards in 2011, but also a Gold and Bronze award for their Steak & Kidney Pie and Chicken, Chilli & Cheese Pasty. They also achieved a Silver Award for the delicatessen at the True Taste of Wales 2011. They offer a wide range of local and continental cheeses, homemade pies, quiches, salads, pâtés, olives and charcuterie, as well as a wide range of Welsh wines, spirits and liqueurs. They also stock Charbonnel et Walker and Booja Booja chocolates alongside chocolates and fudges produced in Wales.

4 Well Street, Ruthin, LL151AE

01824707161
