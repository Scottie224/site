Caffi Florence

Stanley House Hotel and Spa consists of two restaurants, ‘Grill On The Hill’ and ‘Mr Fred’s Bar’. ‘Grill On The Hill’ uses the best Lancashire meat and produce, whilst ‘Mr Fred’s Bar’ is a gastro-inspired menu, ranging from light bites to sharing platters, using local produce.
