Waveney Valley Food Trail

Holton Orchard

Holton Orchard is a family owned apple orchard. In their farm shop, they sell their own apples and plums, as well as fresh fruit and vegetables that are, when possible, sourced from local farmers. They also sell an assortment of groceries, such as milk, eggs, bacon, canned goods and sweets.

Holton Orchards, The Street, Holton, Halesworth, Suffolk

01986 873142

Marybelle Dairy

They source their milk from local family-run farms in Norfolk and Suffolk with an emphasis on low intensity and care for their animals. They then produce their own range of milk, cream, yoghurt and ice cream in their dairy near Halesworth. All of their produce carry the Red Tractor seal of approval, which means that their farms are accredited by SAI Global for high standards of management and animal welfare.

Marybelle Dairy, Walpole, Halesworth, Suffolk, IP19 9AU

01986 784658

Wissett Village Shop

Wissett Village Shop supply a good selection of locally produced meats, cheeses, bread, vegetables. They also have other services available, including dry cleaning, coal, logos, gas, and prescription drop off and pick up point, stamps.

The Wissett Plough, The Street, Wissett, Halesworth, Suffolk, IP19 0JE

01986 872201

Wissett Wines

The main varieties grown here include Pinot Gris, Madeleine Angevine and Auxerrios, and some Pinot Noir and Pinot Meunier. They produce regional, quality still and sparkling wines, which taste good on their own, but also perfectly complement summer salads, fish and seafood dishes.

Valley Farm Vineyards, Wissett, Suffolk, IP19 0JJ

01986 785535
