Carmarthenshire West Food Trail

Albert Rees

Located in a shop at Carmarthens Indoor Market, Albert Rees is a revered ham and bacon curers. Curing all produce within the family, a favourite treat is Carmarthen Streaky Bacon or Carmarthen Back Bacon.

Carmarthen, Indoor Market, SA31 1QY

01267 237687

Cowpots Ice Cream Parlour & Milkshake Bar

A popular ice-cream parlour and milkshake bar in Carmarthen town centre, serving up award-winning ice cream, including Red Cherry Delight Jersey Ice Cream. Make your own cowshake from sweet treats of your own choice.

4 Blue Street, Carmarthen, SA31 3LQ

07531 612877

Etta's Royal Cake

This bakery, based in the village of Llansteffan, makes cake ready for a prince. When Prince Charles tasted their soft fruit cake, the bakery started making its big success.

Richardson Home Bakery, Nolands Lodge Bakery, Llansteffan, SA33 5JZ

01267 241248

Philip Hughes Butcher

A butchers based in the small town of St Clears, turns out up to 7,000 homemade pies a week. Since 1977, Philip Hughes has been sourcing quality meat from the wealth of local farms.

The Butcher Shop, Pentre Road, St Clears, SA33 4AA

01994 230062

Brynderi Honey Farm

At this Honey Farm in the village of Blaenwaun, North of Whitland, eat some honey-flavoured ice-cream whilst hearing about the life-cycle of a bee. The clean air that comes in off the Atlantic, is the suspicious factor for people believing that this honey is top quality.

Brynderi Honey Farm, Blaenwaun, Whitland, SA34 0JD

01994 448653

Taste of the Valleys

Run by a father and son along the street from the Carmarthen Indoor Market every Friday. Produce, including squirrels, wild boar, pigs and beef are sourced from local estates and then hand-prepared by the family at their farm.

Caer Arglwyddes, Pontantwn, Kidwelly, SA17 5LN

07866 938736

Wrights Independent Food Emporium

Selling a large range of produce, from free-range chicken, outdoor reared oxford sandy and black pork, native breed beef to nearly all fruit and veg being organic.

Golden Grove Arms, Llanarthne, SA32 8JU

Browns Hotel

Dylan Thomas favourite pub. He used to give Browns telephone number out at his own when he lived in Laugharne.

King Street, Laugharne, Carmarthenshire, SA33 4RY

Cwrt Bach Farm

The aim of this Farm is to provide fresh and flavour-packed produce to the locals, including the recently added homemade sauces and preserves. With the produce travelling less than 50 metres from the garden to the kitchen, the food miles are low and the flavour is high. Cwrt Bach Farm has received a 2-star gold award at the coveted Great Taste Awards in 2012.

Cefnypant, Whitland, Carmarthenshire, SA34 0TR

01994 419086

From cooked and cured meats and fresh meat being prepared on the premises, this Butcher and Charcuterie offers a high range of local produce.
