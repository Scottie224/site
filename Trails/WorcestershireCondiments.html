Condiments, Preserves and Honey

Berrow's Honey

This honey is collected from both the Severn and Teme valleys using a variety of wild and cultivated sources locally. The bees are then taken to the heather in the autumn. The products available today are, honey mustard, flavoured honeys with zest, including, lemon, lime and orange, honey and garlic and honey and walnuts.

The Berrow, Martley, Worcester, WR6 6PQ

01886 821237

Cotswold Gold Ltd.

Cotswold Gold is a natural 100% British Extra Virgin Rapeseed Oil, which is extracted using traditional cold pressing. This provides a healthy alternative cooking oil for culinary needs. Cotswold Gold is produced in many infusions, ideal for lifting any dish.

East Lodge Farm, Stanton, Broadway, WR12 7NH

07867 938221

Coxon's Kitchen

Two specialist areas at Coxon’s Kitchen are recipe development and culinary innovation. They have created and developed sales of a globally unique Ale-Gar and two historic vinegars.

The Malt House, West Side, North Littleton, Evesham, WR11 8QP

01386 830299

Emily's Jams & Pickles

This is an award-winning, home-run business that specialises in making a variety of jams, marmalades, chutneys, pickles, mustards, oils, dressings and vinegars. These products contain no artificial additives or preservatives and are made in small batches to ensure quality freshness. A sugar free range within the savoury selection is also available.

Lyppard Kettleby, Worcester, WR4 OHZ

01905 611955

Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce

First selling their famous Worcestershire Sauce in 1838, it is now used in many meals today, ranging from Welsh Rarebit to a Bloody Mary. It is suitable for many people as it’s gluten free, doesn’t contain any peanuts, tree-nuts or nut oil of any type; however, it’s not suitable for vegetarians as it contains anchovies.

What-A-Pickle

Camilla Lywood prides herself in producing a small range of products, after just starting the business 5 years ago from a humble home kitchen. These products become ideal as dips, marinades, and an addition for cold meats and cheese, flavourings for stir fries, pasta sauces and gravy.

What a Pickle, Whitbach Farmhouse, Whitbach, Ludlow, Shropshire, SY8 3DU

01584 876694 / 07989 440156
