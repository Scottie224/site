Carmarthenshire East Food Trail

Cnwd

This is a relatively new gourmet restaurant located at the end of the M4. The owner, Scott has returned to his Carmarthenshire roots to create smoked fish and terrines.

Unit 9, Crosshands Business Centre, Heol Parc Mawr, Cross Hands, Llanelli, SA14 6RE

01269 833703

Cwmcerrig Farm Shop & Grill

A popular pit stop for locals to buy local fruit and vegetables, hand-patted Welsh butter and features an award-winning butchery that sells best cuts of the Watkins' own Texel lamb and Hereford beef.

Cwmcerrig Farm, Gorslas, Llanelli, SA14 7HU

01269 844405

Dinefwr Venison

The Venison is reared on an 800 acre, medieval deer park on the Dinefwr estate and is managed by Lead Ranger, Wyn Davies. "I am really proud that the Dinefwr venison has won awards because this is the first time it has been marketed to the public, said Wyn".

01558 825918

Heavenly

Located in the town, Llandeilo, this shop is filled with jars of old-fashioned sweets, chocolate treats, desserts, patisserie and ice cream. The owner uses Welsh organic milk and cream to make no less than 300 recipes and with a weeks notice, will also make any flavour you like. Dogs are even catered for, with a dog friendly variety.

Rhosmaen Street, Llandeilo, SA19 6EN

01558 822800

Nant Du Pork

Rob and Fiona Park are dedicated to the conservation of rare breed Oxford Sandy and Black pigs from their traditional small Welsh Farm. They believe that the pork tastes so good because the pigs live locally in a little arc in an overgrown woodland.

Cwmcrwth Farm, Broad Oak, Llandeilo, SA32 8QP

01558 669160

Cothi Valley Goats

Making all produce fresh from the goats, including Welsh haloumy, soft and hard cheeses and bespoke cheeses. They also make great yoghurt drinks, quiches, Kid meat pies and more from the farmhouse kitchen.

Cilwr Farm, Tally, Llandeilo, Carmarthenshire, SA19 7BQ

01558 685555

Llanelli Market

A traditional indoor market to buy your fish and seafood, including local cockles and laverbread. The market also has many other stalls from clothes to electrical goods.

Olive Branch Deli

This deli has everything from local veg and foods to truffle butter. The deli also stocks local gourmet chutneys, jams and a selection of produce from one of their food heroes, Scott at Cnwd.

1 Market Street, Llandeilo, SA19 6AH

01558 823030

Penygawse Guesthouse and Tearooms

A traditional tea room, with good coffee from real baristas and a selection of homemade cakes and sandwiches. They have a signature coffee latte, marsaccino (coffee, mars, chocolate) and mintyccino (coffee, after 8 chocolate).

12 High St, Llandovery, Carmarthenshire, SA20 0PU

01550 721727

Sosban

Described as one of the jewels in Llanellis crown, Sosban are the winners of the AA Welsh Restaurant of the Year 2012. Located in the old Pumphouse at Llanellis newly restored North Dock. The head chef prides herself in serving only the finest local produce, from local seafood and lamb.

North Dock, Llanelli, Carmarthenshire, SA15 2LF

01554 270020

Tafarn Morlais

Food is served 7 days a week, with Sky Sports shown live and live entertainment every Saturday night. Real ales, a large selection of wines, a beer garden, used for corporate parties and barbeques. Areas are available for private parties and buffets.

1 Pontardulais Rd, Llangennech, Llanelli, Carmarthenshire, SA4 8YE

01554 820121
