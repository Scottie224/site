Meat, Poultry and Game

Checketts Fine Foods

This is a long established family butchers and have been at their current shop since 1925. Their meat and poultry is sourced from local farms and there’s also a fish counter with probably the largest selection of fresh fish in the area.

Church Villa, Main Road, Ombersley, NR. Worcester, WR9 0EW

01905 620750

Collins of Broadway.

This is a long established family butcher, selling groceries, seasonal fruit and vegetables, as well as local meat, poultry and game.

Kennel Lane, High Street, Broadway, Worcestershire, WR12 7DP

01386 852061

Eckington Manor

This working farm offers bed and breakfast and a state-of-the-art cookery schools. The on-site Cook Shop sells the home-bred meat, including Aberdeen Angus and Highland Beef, plus other quality produce and cookware. Some of the condiments are home produced and the cheese is locally sourced.

Hammock Road, Eckington, Worcestershire, WR10 3BJ

01386 751600

Havard & Co

These are producers of the finest rare breed lamb and mutton, from the pastures, direct to your door. The meat is hung for at least a week to ensure maximum tenderness and smoothness of texture.

Phepson Farm, Himbleton, Droitwich Spa, WR9 7JZ

01905 391205

Kite's Nest Farm

This brother-sister partnership put animal welfare as their top priority with full traceability. Emphasis is made on protecting the environment and all wildlife, selling beef joints, steak, mince and 100% beef burgers.

Snowshill Road, Broadway, Worcestershire, WR12 7JT

01386 853320

Lickhill Farm Meats

Priding themselves in producing top quality meat, they keep traditional breeds of South Devon beef cattle and Jacob sheep which they handle themselves. The farm is run as naturally as possible with cattle and sheep out at grass for most of the year. All meat sold is home reared and well hung for taste and tenderness.

Woodgreen Farm, 1 Lickhill Road North, Stourport-on-Severn, Worcestershire, DY13 8RW

01299 829589

Muddy Boots Real Foods

Using Aberdeen Angus beef to make beef burgers and the coming up with new flavours, such as caramelised onion and mozzarella or cider and stilton to create a versatile family meal.

Church Farm, Shrawley, Nr. Worcester, WR6 6TS

01905 622899
