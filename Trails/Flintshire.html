Piccadilly

This restaurant and bar located in the Town of Caerwys, have crafted a menu that supports many local farmers, so that their provisions and the food miles are as low as possible. Top food is served in the Grand Stand Restaurant, parties are hosted in the private room and many drinks and local ales are available in the Jockeys Lounge.

North Street Caerwys, Mold, Flintshire, CH7 5AW
