Hartnells Fresh Foods

Hartnells Fresh Foods are an independent butchers and bakers. They make a large range of their products in the shop, which include a variety of Sausages. Hartnells also sell home-made burgers, including Beef (gluten free beef only), Pork and Apple and many more. The Bakery sells freshly baked baguettes and rolls made daily. There are homemade pies and pasties, cakes and flapjacks baked on the premises.

Seasons

Seasons are a unique, well established and independent health food shop in Exeter that has been serving the local community since 1978. Seasons is an Aladdin’s cave of culinary delights selling local food from Devon and beyond, specialising in organic food produced in the local rural area.

Martin's Sandwiches

We’ve been making great food locally since 1983, that’s well over a quarter of a century! Our dedicated team of workers take great care in handcrafting each sandwich. Because we don’t use a conveyor belt system with buttering machines and depositors we are able to make small batches of one off fillings which can be for specific individual needs, for example gluten free, halal, vegan etc

The Real Food Store

We are a unique, independent, community-owned store, bakery and cafe, with more than 300 shareholders who came together to ensure that it would be possible to establish a shop that has brought fresh produce back to the city centre We stock a wide range of ‘larder goods’ – oats, dried fruits, pasta, and rice – as well as locally produced cheeses, yoghurt, pickles and honey. Alongside these essentials we stock beers from local micro-breweries, Sandford Orchards award winning ciders and a great range of Luscombe’s soft drinks and juices.

Gibsons Plaice Fishmongers

Established in 1992 Gibsons Plaice is a traditional wet fishmongers supplying fresh local fish to the general public and the trade. They buy the majority of the fish from the smaller day boats landing into Brixham and Exmouth. Due to seasonal variations of the fishing this means they have in excess of 50 different species of fish and shellfish to choose from over the course of a year.

38 Magdalen Road EX2 4TD

01392 495344

Burrow Farm at Courtneys

Burrow Farm at Courtneys is a traditional Butchers shop, supplying traditional Devon meats. The award-winning Red Ruby Devon beef is from their own farm on the nearby Killerton Estate in Broadclyst and the lamb, pork and chicken is all supplied from local farms. The butchers make all of their own sausages and hogs puddings and prepare all joints of meat on the premises They offer a personal service, supplying your favourite cuts of meat just the way you like it.
