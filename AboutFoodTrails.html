About Food Trails

Food trails are pre-planned holiday travel routes for you to take to sample local cuisine.

Throughout the UK are hundreds of local, family run businesses such as breweries, butchers and dairies which provide delicious, high quality, unique produce that rely on these food trails to spread word about their business.

They're fantastic and easy to do, all you need is a car, some time off and a map and you're on your way!
